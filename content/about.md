      <h2 class="section-title">Who I Am</h2>
      <div class="prose">
        <p>A psychological counsellor with a Master's degree in Counselling, practising in both French and English in Zurich and online. My work is shaped by a genuine curiosity about the human experience and a deep commitment to providing a safe, accepting space for all my clients.</p>

        <p>My own experience of living across different countries and cultures has given me first-hand insight into the disorientation and richness of life in transition. This personal journey informs my work with expats, international families, and anyone navigating questions of identity and belonging.</p>

        <h2>My Approach to Counselling</h2>
        <p>I practise primarily from a person-centred perspective, which means I believe you are the expert on your own life. My role is not to give advice or direct you towards particular outcomes, but to offer the conditions — unconditional positive regard, empathy, and authenticity — in which meaningful growth becomes possible.</p>

        <p>I draw flexibly on other evidence-based approaches, including Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), Emotion Focused Therapy (EFT), mindfulness-based techniques, and psychoeducation, tailoring my approach to what is most useful for you in any given moment.</p>

        <blockquote>"I believe that within every person lies the capacity to flourish — and that the right support, at the right time, can unlock that potential."</blockquote>

        <h2>My Specialisms</h2>
        <p>Over the course of my training and practice, I have developed particular expertise in three interconnected areas:</p>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>Life transitions and identity</strong> — supporting people through the major shifts that reshape who we are</li>
          <li><strong>ADHD and neurodiversity</strong> — moving from deficit-based thinking to strengths-based living</li>
          <li><strong>Digital literacy and technology wellbeing</strong> — navigating the psychological impacts of our digital world</li>
        </ul>

        <h2>Professional Memberships &amp; Ethics</h2>
        <p>I am a registered member of the SGfB (Schweizer Gesellschaft für Beratung), the ACA (Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy), and the BACP (British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy). I adhere to all three organisations' codes of ethics and engage in regular supervision and continuing professional development.</p>

        <p>All sessions are fully confidential. As a member of these professional bodies, I operate within a clear ethical framework designed to protect your wellbeing and our therapeutic relationship.</p>

        <h2>A Bilingual Practice</h2>
        <p>I offer counselling in both <strong>English and French</strong>. For many of my clients, being able to explore their inner world in their first language — or in the language that best captures a particular experience — makes a profound difference. You are welcome to switch between languages within sessions if that feels most natural to you.</p>
